how it’s made?

Our ice cream factory is located in a unique place - near the purest mountain river and a picturesque valley with pastures on which our cows graze. Thanks to the unsurpassed nature and the careful care of farmers, we collect the best milk for ice cream production.

Our ice cream is made only from fresh milk and organic fruits. In the best traditions and with love, we create masterpieces that will allow you to enjoy the wonderful taste of natural, fruity and creamy ice cream.
